Get Foxy

Send and receive money from your wallet.

Foxy is a streamlined digital wallet designed to make peer-to-peer payments simple, secure, and accessible. Built with ease of use in mind, Foxy allows users to send, receive, and manage digital assets directly from their mobile devices. With a focus on transparency and control, Foxy operates as a non-custodial wallet, meaning users maintain full ownership of their funds and private keys. Whether you're splitting bills, paying friends, or exploring digital currencies, Foxy provides the essential tools to handle payments effortlessly.
